LIBF EXPO - 2024

The Lohana International Business Forum (LIBF) Expo 2024, held from January 18 to 21 at the Helipad Exhibition Center in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, showcased the global business acumen of the Lohana community. The expo connects Indian businesses with global players and attracts over 200 exhibitors from over 30 countries across sectors such as real estate, BFSI, healthcare, technology education and FMCG. The high profile of the event was evident with the attendance including Gujarat Industries Minister Shri Balvantsinh Rajput, former ISRO Chairman and actor and businessman Vivek Oberoi Shri AS Kiran Kumar.

Euro Exim Bank, as the title sponsor, highlighted the expo's focus on fostering global partnerships. The event served as a platform for over 34 sectors, promoting collaboration and innovation. Noteworthy participants such as government officials, industry tycoons, scholars, and start-ups underscored the diverse and influential nature of the audience.
