HYDROPHONICS CONSULTING SERVICES

Hydroponics or soilless farming, a sustainable farming technique achieved great attention to encounter the problems and limitations of conventional soil-based farming. Hydroponics farming practices ensure the production of a variety of healthy crops by prioritizing sustainability with minimal water usage and reduced environmental footprints.
EEB Engineering Design and Consultancy Services Pvt Ltd offers exceptional and modern innovations which will help you to experience the wonder of hydroponics technology to grow your desired crops and vegetables. Our hydroponics experts, having immense experience in the field, provide training and consultancy services throughout your hydroponics cultivation journey.

We have recently set up a 3000 sq.ft semi-automated demo roof top greenhouse to grow exotic vegetables hydroponically. We also have a dedicated R & D polyhouse dedicatedly set up to design, test hydroponic systems and nutrient management. Our team is actively experimenting with various hydroponic growing systems viz. NFT systems (flat bed and A-frames), aggregate systems, Dutch buckets, Microgreens and have successfully grown cherry tomatoes, lettuce, basil, chilies, kale etc., Our vertical farming set up has successfully grown and harvested turmeric at the finest quality with high curcumin content.

At EEBEDCS, we offer complete hydroponic consultancy services to assist our customers to achieve their farming aspirations. Our well-versed hydroponic consultancy team provides complete support and guidance in developing customized farm plans to meet customer needs and budget.

The hydroponic consultancy provides end to end solutions to make your hydroponics farm into function with services including.

Feasibility studies and farm design
Selection of equipment and installation.
Crop selection and management
Optimization of nutrients for crop development.
Environment, climate control and monitoring.
Integrated pest and disease management.
Strategy planning for sales and marketing.
Financial planning
Endless training and education
